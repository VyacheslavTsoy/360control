Training

Basic training

This course provides captains and mates an excellent opportunity to learn the basic skills to control and manoeuvre a tug in a save and efficient way. In a step by step program, the trainee will learn and understand the basics of the different free sailing, towage and assisting methods.

Discover more Check training dates Apply

Advanced training

This course provides captains and mates an excellent opportunity to train more complicated tug manoeuvring scenarios in a save and efficient way. In a step by step program, the participant will become more comfortable with complex situations and improve the situational awareness during tug operations.

Discover more Apply

Tailor made training

Depending on the specific needs of the participants, 360-Control can provide tailor made training programs. Specific scenarios, company case studies, assessments or exams are possible options for a tailor-made program.

On-the-job training

Complementary to the tug simulator training, practical training sessions on board a real tug can be implemented in the training schedule.

See ‘on-the-job training’ for more information

Assessment

Manoeuvring skills assessment

A step by step simulator assessment program has been developed to perform an objective assessment on the tug manoeuvring skills of an individual person.
In preparation for the assessment detailed information regarding the required level of competence is required.

Personal assessment

On request personal simulator assessments can be performed. Personal assessments will be executed under guidance of a certified (NIP) psychologist.

Approach

To make sure the acquired knowledge and skills are implemented within your organization. 360-Control performs an analysis of the current situation of knowledge and skills and consequently offers a suitable standard or tailor-made training.

INTAKE
A 360-Control assesses and reports the customer requirements and the level of knowledge and skills present before the tug-master-training.
CUSTOMER VISION
The customer describes the level of knowledge and skills that are required to operate and possibly helps to formulate all learning objectives.
TRAINING PROPOSAL
360-Control creates a training-proposal to comply with the customer’s needs and learning objectives.
TRAINING Agreement
Execution of tug-master training by 360-Control Instructors in classroom and the simulator and/or on-the job training.
TRAINING EXECUTION
Execution of tug-master training by 360-Control Instructors in classroom and the simulator and/or on-the job training.
TRAINING EVALUATION
Knowledge and skill level is assessed to a standard set of criteria and certificated too knowledge and skill levels that are presented.
FOLLOW-UP
After generally the training 360-Control will contact the customer

Simulator training

The use of a very realistic high-end tug simulator with an outside field of view of 360° combined with identical Damen ASD 2810/3212 bridge and navigation equipment creates a very strong learning environment.

Practical training

Complementary to the tug simulator training, practical training sessions on board a Damen ASD 2810 tug can be implemented in the training schedule. The daily operations, tug management and the way of life on board can be seen in practice. In between the tug operations the trainee will take the controls to manoeuvre the free sailing tug.

ON THE JOB TRAINING

Practical training session on board a real ASD tug can be implemented* into the training
schedule, complementary to the simulator training.

Advantages of on-the-job-training are:

the chance to experience the real tugboat behaviour
the insight of the life, the daily operations and management on board the tugboat
that the trainee will be convinced of the realistic level of our ASD simulator training

The practical sessions will normally take place in the area of the port of
Amsterdam.

*Depending on the availability of an ASD tug
